Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Overview

RunKcal ("we", "us", or "our") operates runkcal.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our free calorie calculators.

2. Information We Collect

RunKcal does not require account creation or personal registration. We do not store the weight, distance, or pace values you enter into our calculators — all calculations happen in your browser.

We automatically collect limited technical information through third-party services including:

• Google Analytics 4 — anonymous usage statistics (pages visited, session duration, approximate geographic region). No personally identifiable information is collected. Google Privacy Policy.

• Google AdSense — advertising cookies to show relevant ads. Google may use your browsing data to personalize ads. Google Ads Policy.

• Cloudflare — our hosting provider may log IP addresses for security and performance. Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These are third-party cookies used for analytics and advertising purposes. You can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. How We Use Information

Anonymous analytics data is used solely to understand how visitors use our site and improve our calculators. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with any third parties beyond the analytics and advertising services described above.

5. Children's Privacy

RunKcal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we do not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data held by Google. Contact us at hello@runkcal.com with any privacy concerns.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at hello@runkcal.com.
